Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Skinmaxxing ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

When you use Skinmaxxing, the following data is collected and stored locally on your device:

Profile information: Age range, skin concerns, diet, water intake, and skincare routine preferences you provide during onboarding.

Skin scan results: Your skin analysis scores, category breakdowns, and personalized advice generated by the app.

Photos: Photos you take or upload for skin analysis are stored locally on your device.

Daily logs: Checklist completion, streaks, and skincare routine tracking data.

Chat history: Messages you send to the AI Skin Coach are stored locally on your device (up to 50 messages).

2. Face Data — Collection, Use, and Retention

Because our app analyzes facial skin, we want to be fully transparent about how face data is handled.

2.1 What face data we collect

When you use the skin scan feature, the app captures or allows you to upload a photo of your face. This photo contains facial image data which is used solely for the purpose of AI-powered skin analysis.

2.2 How face data is used

Your face photo is used for one purpose only: to analyze your skin condition and generate a skin score with category breakdowns (Acne, Texture, Pores, Dark Spots, Oiliness, Redness, Hydration, and Overall Tone). The photo is sent to Google's Gemini AI API along with your profile information (age range, skin concerns, skin type) so the AI can generate a personalized analysis. No facial recognition, facial mapping, or biometric identification is performed. The app only analyzes visible skin conditions.

2.3 Third-party sharing of face data

Your face photo is sent to Google's Gemini AI API over a secure HTTPS connection for analysis. Google processes this data in accordance with their own privacy policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not send your face data to any other third party. We do not use your face data for advertising, tracking, or any purpose other than generating your skin analysis.

2.4 Where face data is stored

Your face photo is stored locally on your device only. It is never stored on our servers because we do not operate any servers. When the photo is sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis, it is transmitted over HTTPS and used only to generate the analysis response. We do not retain any copy of the photo after the API response is received.

2.5 How long face data is retained

The face photo remains on your device for as long as you keep the app installed and do not delete your data. You can delete all face data at any time by going to Settings → Delete All Data within the app, or by deleting the app entirely. We do not retain any face data on any server at any time.

3. AI Skin Coach Data

When you chat with the AI Skin Coach, your messages along with your skin scores and profile information are sent to Google's Gemini AI API to generate personalized responses. No chat data is stored on any external server. Chat history is stored locally on your device only (up to 50 messages).

4. Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI API: Used to analyze skin photos and power the AI coach. The following data is sent to Google when you use these features:

— For skin scans: your face photo (image data) and profile information (age range, skin concerns, skin type)

— For AI coach: your text messages, skin scores, and profile information

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Google's privacy policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy) applies to their processing of this data.

RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat processes transaction data in accordance with their privacy policy. No personal health data or face data is shared with RevenueCat.

Apple: In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any personally identifiable information. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We do not perform facial recognition or biometric identification.

6. User Consent

Before your first skin scan, the app asks for your explicit consent to send your face photo to Google's Gemini AI API for analysis. You may decline, in which case the scan feature will not function. By proceeding with a scan, you consent to your face photo being sent to Google's Gemini AI API for the sole purpose of generating your skin analysis.

7. Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard device storage. Photos remain on your device and are only temporarily transmitted to Google's Gemini AI API for analysis. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

Access: All your data is stored on your device and accessible to you at any time within the app.

Deletion: You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings and tapping "Delete all data". You can also delete the app to remove all stored data.

Portability: Your data is stored locally and can be removed by deleting the app.

Objection: You can stop using the app at any time. No data processing occurs without your active use of the app.

9. Children's Privacy

Skinmaxxing is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us and we will assist with data deletion.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
