Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Skinmaxxing ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

When you use Skinmaxxing, the following data is collected and stored locally on your device:

Profile information: Age range, skin concerns, diet, water intake, and skincare routine preferences you provide during onboarding.

Skin scan results: Your skin analysis scores, category breakdowns, and personalized advice generated by the app.

Photos: Photos you take or upload for skin analysis are stored locally on your device.

Daily logs: Checklist completion, streaks, and skincare routine tracking data.

Chat history: Messages you send to the AI Skin Coach are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Data

Skin analysis: When you scan your skin, your photo is sent to Google's Gemini API over a secure HTTPS connection for analysis. The photo is used solely to generate your skin report and is not stored by us after the response is received. Google's use of this data is governed by their own privacy policy.

AI Skin Coach: When you chat with the AI coach, your messages and profile information are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate personalized responses. No chat data is stored on our servers.

All other data (profile, scan results, logs, streaks) is stored exclusively on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any personally identifiable information. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Google Gemini API: Used to analyze skin photos and power the AI coach. Photos and messages are transmitted over HTTPS. Google's privacy policy applies to their processing of this data.

RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat processes transaction data in accordance with their privacy policy. No personal health data is shared with RevenueCat.

Apple: In-app purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

5. Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard device storage. Photos remain on your device and are only temporarily transmitted for analysis. We do not operate servers that store your personal data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

Access: All your data is stored on your device and accessible to you at any time within the app.

Deletion: You can delete all your data at any time by going to Settings and tapping "Delete all data". You can also delete the app to remove all stored data.

Portability: Your data is stored locally and can be removed by deleting the app.

Objection: You can stop using the app at any time. No data processing occurs without your active use of the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Skinmaxxing is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us and we will assist with data deletion.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
